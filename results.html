To verify the changes made to the model, let's compare it to the old model. As we have seen previously, for a flat $\mathrm{PSD}$ over the filter channel bandwidth, the old model and the new model are equivalent. We therefore expect the updates to only affect those positions where the signal that reaches the detectors is far from constant over the entire Lorentzian filter curve.

Noise Equivalent Power

Let's first take a look at how the changes affected the noise equivalent power.

For the simulated case this figure shows exactly what we expected: when the incoming power is at a local maximum, the new $\mathrm{NEP}$ is much lower than before.
